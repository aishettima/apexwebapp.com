Data protection

We only process personal data for the purpose of providing a functional and user-friendly website, including its content and the services offered . According to Art. 4 No. 1 of Regulation (EU) 2016/679, i.e. the General Data Protection Regulation (hereinafter referred to as “GDPR”) "personal data" means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person ("data subject"); an identifiable natural person is one who can be identified, directly or indirectly, in particular by reference to an identifier such as a name, an identification number, location data, an online identifier or to one or more factors specific to the physical, physiological, genetic, mental, economic, cultural or social identity of that natural person.

According to Art. 4 No. 2 of Regulation (EU) 2016/679, Data processing is the process of collection and proceesing data through collecting, sorting, storing, changing, querying, disclosing, distribution, comparision, deleting or destroying of data. We hereby inform you about the basis and duration of processing of personal data including through third components. The third parties are liable to their responsibility of processing their data. But we will add the links to the third party data policy which will enable you to understand how they process their data. Our privacy policy include the information about us, the right of users and those that might be affected.

Cookies

1) Session-Cookies : We use cookies on our website. Cookies are small text files or other storage technologies which are stored on your end device by the Internet browser you use. These cookies process some kind of information about the visitor to our website, such as your browser or location data or your IP address. This use of cookies makes our website more user-friendly, effective and mores secure, because the processing enables, our website to be displayed in different languages . The legal basis for this processing is (Art. 6 Para. 1 lit b.) GDPR, as long as these cookies are used to process data for contract initiation or contract execution. If the processing does not serve to initiate or process a contract, our legitimate interest lies in improving the functionality of our website. The legal basis is then (Article 6 Paragraph 1 Letter f) GDPR. These session cookies are deleted when you close your internet browser.


2) Third-party cookies Often times, our website also uses cookies from other companies with whom we work together. See below for detail information:

3) Restriction of cookies If you restrict or prevent the use of cookies, you will not get the best functonalities of our website. Cookies can be prevented or restricted by setting your internet browser. Also cookies that have already been saved can be removed or deleted at anytime. This however depends on the internet browser you are using. For more help about this visit the help section of your internet browser or contact the internet browser provider. This is also true for Flash cookies. But for Flash cookies, processing can not be stopped via setting of the internet browser. To resolve this you must alter the settings of Flash player. If you need help about this use the documentation of your flash player or do contact the manufacturer.
